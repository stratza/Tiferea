FIRST STEPS - read this once:
Open the console and create the admin account (first run). After that, users
sign in or continue as a read-only Viewer; roles Admin/Operator/Viewer are
enforced server-side. TifEra terminates no TLS, so front any exposure beyond
local port-forward with a TLS proxy (session cookies/passwords cross the wire).
